Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

What are the delivery times?

Orders are generally delivered within 12 to 18 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on the destination and periods of high demand (holidays, new releases). You will receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order is shipped.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number. You can use this number on our Track Order page or directly on the carrier's website. If you haven't received a tracking number within 5 business days, feel free to contact us.

What is the difference between Fan Version and Match Version?

The Fan Version is a high-quality replica designed for everyday wear and fan use. It features a relaxed, comfortable fit. The Match Version is a player-cut jersey made with Dri-fit technology — closer to the pitch-worn version. It is lighter, more fitted, and ideal for playing or collectors. The Match Version is $10 more.

Can I customize my jersey with a name and number?

Yes! When adding a jersey to your cart, simply select the 'With Flocking' option and enter the name and number of your choice. Flocking (customization) costs $5 extra. Please note: customized jerseys are not refundable unless there is an error or defect on our part.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal (all countries) and Interac e-Transfer (Canada only). PayPal: elevenfcstore@outlook.com. Interac: elevenfcstore@outlook.com. All transactions are secured.

How do I choose my size?

For jerseys and tracksuits, we use standard S / M / L / XL / XXL sizing. If you're between two sizes, we recommend going up. Kids' jerseys are available in sizes 16 to 28. A detailed size guide will be available soon on our Size Guide page.

What if I received the wrong product?

If you received a wrong or defective product, contact us within 7 days at elevenfcstore@outlook.com. Include your order number and photos of the product. We will process a replacement or full refund within 5 to 10 business days after verification.

Still have questions? Our team is here to help.

Official Policy

Returns & Refunds

Our complete return and refund policy. For any questions: elevenfcstore@outlook.com

Return Window

Returns are accepted within 7 days of receiving your order.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, the product must:

  • Be new and unused
  • Not have been worn
  • Not have been washed
  • Be returned in its original condition
  • Have all original tags and packaging intact

Personalized Products

Personalized products are not eligible for return or refund. This includes:

  • Custom flocking
  • Custom player name
  • Custom player number
Exception

A return or refund will be accepted if the product received is incorrect, defective, or the result of a preparation error on our part.

Defective Products or Order Errors

If you receive a defective product, an incorrect product, or a wrong size sent by Eleven FC Store, we will offer a replacement or full refund after verification. This applies to:

  • Defective product (manufacturing defect)
  • Wrong product received
  • Wrong size sent by Eleven FC Store

Return Shipping Costs

  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.
  • Exception: if the return is due to an order error or a defective product on our part, Eleven FC Store covers all return shipping costs.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 10 business days after receipt and inspection of the returned product.

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us by email at elevenfcstore@outlook.com within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Include your order number, photos of the product, and the reason for your return.
  3. We will confirm eligibility and provide return instructions within 48 hours.
  4. Ship the item back in its original packaging.
  5. Once received and inspected, your refund or replacement will be processed within 5 to 10 business days.
